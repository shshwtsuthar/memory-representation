```
CMD_FRAC:

The grammar recognizes three variants: `\frac`, `\dfrac` (display style), and `\tfrac` (text style).

**Frac Rule (Lines 248-249):**
```
frac: CMD_FRAC (upperd = DIGIT | L_BRACE upper = expr R_BRACE)

```

This rule allows fractions to have either:
- Simple digit numerators/denominators (like `\frac12`)
- Full expressions in braces (like `\frac{x+1}{y}`)
